💡 Setup Guide & Best Practices

  • Before Sync (Avoid Conflicts): Make sure the option "Automatically add to collection" is disabled in your library metadata settings (Dashboard → Libraries → click your library) to prevent TMDB from interfering.
  • After Sync (Group Movies): To group movies under their collection cards within the main movie library view, you must enable "Group movies into collections" under Dashboard → Libraries → Display.
